Gundog Trials

Canterbury Kennel Association is the only All Breeds Club in New Zealand licensed to hold Championship Gundog Trials.

CKA partners with the Canterbury Gun Dog Club to conduct championship-level gundog trials throughout the year. These trials are open to a wide range of gundog breeds competing across several championship divisions.

Competition Divisions

Championship Divisions

  • All Breeds
  • Pointer & Setter
  • Spaniel
  • Retriever

Minor Trial Divisions

  • Puppy
  • Novice
  • Limit

Easter Championship Trial Results

Results from our Easter Championship Trial are published here each year. Results list handler names, dog names, breed designations, and final rankings for all qualifying competitors.

For the most recent trial results or to enquire about entering, contact the Canterbury Gun Dog Club.
