Canterbury Kennel Centre

The Canterbury Kennel Centre is located at 701 McLeans Island Road, Christchurch 8051. The venue is available for hire by breed clubs, multi-breed clubs, and all-breeds clubs for shows, ribbon parades, training, and meetings.

All bookings must be confirmed with the Venue Coordinator before publicising your event. Contact: bookings@cka.co.nz
