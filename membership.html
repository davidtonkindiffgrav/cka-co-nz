Membership

The Canterbury Kennel Association welcomes new members. Membership supports our shows, training programs, and the ongoing development of the Canterbury Kennel Centre.

How to Join

  1. Complete the membership application form.
  2. Pay the membership fee online.
  3. Submit the completed form to the Secretary for committee confirmation.
Membership applications require online payment plus submission of the membership form to the Secretary. Your application will be confirmed by the committee at their next meeting.

Membership Benefits

  • Voting rights at Annual General Meetings
  • Access to member rates for venue hire and show entries
  • Participation in club training events
  • Eligibility to serve on the committee
  • Regular club newsletters and updates
