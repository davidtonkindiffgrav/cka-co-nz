Canterbury Kennel Association

Ad Homines Docendos De Salute Canum — For teaching people about the welfare of dogs

One of New Zealand’s leading all-breeds kennel clubs, based at the Canterbury Kennel Centre, 701 McLeans Island Road, Christchurch. We promote responsible dog ownership, conduct championship and open shows, and provide training programs for dogs and their owners.
