Competition Categories

Our championship shows feature the following award categories:

  • Best in Show and Reserve Best in Show
  • Best Baby Puppy, Best Puppy, Best Junior, Best Intermediate
  • Best New Zealand Bred
  • Best Open and Best Neuter
  • Special stakes: Puppy Stakes, Veteran Stakes, Grand Veteran Stakes
  • Group awards across all seven breed groups
