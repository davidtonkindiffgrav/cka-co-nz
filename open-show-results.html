Award Categories

Each Open Show awards placements in the following categories:

  • Best in Show & Reserve Best in Show
  • Age classes: Baby Puppy, Puppy, Junior, Intermediate, Open, Limit
  • Breed groups: Toy, Terrier, Gundog, Hound, Working Dog, Utility, Non-Sporting
  • Best New Zealand Bred
