Project History (2007–2025)

Over 111 projects have been completed since 2007, including:

  • Facility upgrades (roofing, guttering, flooring)
  • Amenity installations (shade sails, LED lighting, heat pumps)
  • Equipment purchases (mowers, quad bikes, kitchen appliances)
  • Infrastructure repairs (septic systems, fencing, electrical work)
  • Dog exercise park construction (completed 2013)
