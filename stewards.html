Stewards’ Training Course

If you are interested in attending a Stewarding course — whether as a beginner or to refresh or extend your knowledge — you are cordially invited to participate in our training programme.

Course Overview

Detail Information
Duration 4 weeks
Meeting time Monday evenings, 7:30pm
Location Kennel Centre, 701 McLeans Island Road, Christchurch 8051
Practical component Final session: Ribbon Parade (hands-on stewarding)
Registration fee $5 (covers materials)
Outcome Certificate upon successful completion

What to Bring

  • Pen and paper
  • Current NZKC Show Rules
Enrolment is limited — contact us early to secure your place.

Who Should Attend?

This course is suitable for:

  • Complete beginners who want to learn to steward at dog shows
  • Experienced stewards wishing to refresh their knowledge
  • Those wanting to extend their stewarding skills and confidence
